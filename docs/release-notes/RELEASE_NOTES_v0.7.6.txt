Scope

What changed

- Fixed the Brooklands source-transcript recall weakness in current benchmark
  evidence for the affected local medium lanes.
- Benchmark-mode Local Whisper transcription now uses tracked
  `expected_named_entities` as a narrow `initial_prompt` when those benchmark
  hints are explicitly supplied.
- The Brooklands-focused rerun now preserves literal `Brooklands` in the raw
  source transcript on both `local-medium-gpu` and `local-medium-cpu`.
- Preserved the already-fixed Hybrid translation boundary: the rerun Hybrid
  control still validates `accepted`, and `Brooklands -> Brooklyn` remains
  fixed.
- Preserved raw transcript integrity. The branch does not silently rewrite
  transcript artifacts after transcription.

Why this matters

- The benchmark framework is supposed to catch real quality regressions, not
  leave known proper nouns misheard on the most important recurring control.
- This patch lifts Brooklands recall in the benchmark transcription path
  without reopening the v0.7.4 translation bug or faking the transcript output.
- Operators and reviewers can now read benchmark results without confusing
  source-transcript weakness with translation corruption.
