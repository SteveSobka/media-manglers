Video Mangler helper deployment notes
====================================

Developer box helper copy:

  D:\DATA\CODE\HELPERS\Video Mangler.ps1
  D:\DATA\CODE\HELPERS\Video Mangler.cmd

Run from the helper folder:

  .\Video Mangler.cmd

Or directly:

  powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File "D:\DATA\CODE\HELPERS\Video Mangler.ps1"

Show the helper version:

  powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File "D:\DATA\CODE\HELPERS\Video Mangler.ps1" -Version
